Audience

Our target audience are small local business. Local communities who want to enter the crypto world and start providing its services using cryptocurrencies. We provide information about the process of adopting this technology, and how it can lead to a bigger growth for businesses.

Persona 1

Peter Parker is a small business owner. He owns a local hardware store that provides its products and services to a local community. He wants to show his customers how cryptocurrencies adoption could benefy their community, that's why he looked for our website.

Persona 2

Mary Jane is a local entrepreneur that sells her products online. She knows that adopting cryptocurrencies as payment methods will increase her sales. She wants to provide her clients as much freedom as she can.

Scenarios

  • How prices work with cryptocurrencies?
  • Which crypto should I use for payments?
  • How can I announce the implementation of crypto in my business?
  • How can I set up a wallet?
  • How do taxes work with crypto?
